Another hero on video. This time [Ward Cunningham](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ward_Cunningham), the inventor of the [WikiWikiWeb](http://c2.com/cgi/wiki), interviewed by John Gage of Sun Microsystems at the [Computer History Museum](http://www.computerhistory.org/). As well as [Wiki](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wiki), Ward discusses other key developments, such as [Class-Resources-Collaboration (CRC)](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Class-Responsibility-Collaboration_card) cards, [Software Design Patterns](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Software_design_patterns) and [Extreme Programming](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extreme_programming).
